Privacy Policy

Official Document • Smart Brain Logic Memory

🛡️ 1. Introduction

Welcome to Smart Brain Logic Memory by LogicLabsStudios. We are dedicated to providing a secure, offline gaming experience. This policy explains our zero-data collection approach.

🚫 2. Zero Personal Data Collection

Your privacy is our priority. As an offline board game, we do not collect, track, or share any of your personal information (such as name, email, phone number, or location).

💾 3. Local Game Storage

All your hard-earned high scores, level progress, and game preferences stay exactly where they belong—on your device. We do not use remote servers to store your game data.

📱 4. Third-Party Services

We may use standard third-party services (like Google Play Services) necessary for app functionality or serving ads. These services operate under their own strict privacy policies and only collect anonymous device identifiers.

🗑️ 5. Data Deletion Policy

Since we hold absolutely no data on our servers, you have full control. To completely erase your game data, simply:

  • Go to your device Settings > Apps > Smart Brain Logic Memory > Storage > Clear Data.
  • Or, uninstall the game from your device.

👶 6. Safe for Children (COPPA)

Our game is designed to challenge brains of all ages safely. We do not knowingly collect any data from children under 13.
